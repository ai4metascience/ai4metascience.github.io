Proposed Official Review Form (OpenReview)

AI for Meta-Science workshop @ NeurIPS 2026 · for organizing-team feedback before deployment. Implements the policy agreed in the CFP thread: BayLearn-style grading, detailed justification required only for rejections, full-text attestation, AI assistance allowed with full reviewer responsibility.

1. Title (required)

Brief summary of your review. Free text, max 500 characters.

2. Review (required)

Please evaluate the quality, clarity, and significance of this work, including a list of its pros and cons. A detailed justification is REQUIRED if your recommendation is Reject; for accept recommendations a detailed review is optional but appreciated. You are only obliged to read the main text (not the appendix), and you may use AI assistance (e.g., to check references and appendices), but you carry full responsibility for every claim in this review.

Free text; Markdown and LaTeX supported.

3. Rating (required — select one)

2Accept (oral quality)
1Accept (poster quality)
0Reject (detailed justification required in the review text)

4. Confidence (required — select one)

5Absolutely certain the evaluation is correct; very familiar with the relevant literature
4Confident but not absolutely certain that the evaluation is correct
3Fairly confident that the evaluation is correct
2Willing to defend the evaluation, but quite likely did not understand central parts of the paper
1The evaluation is an educated guess

5. Full-text attestation (required — single checkbox)

☐ I confirm that I have read the full main text of this submission, and I take full responsibility for this review, including any parts produced with AI assistance.

6. AI assistance disclosure (optional)

Briefly describe any AI assistance you used while preparing this review (e.g., checking references, verifying appendices, improving wording). This is encouraged for transparency and does not affect how your review is weighted.

Free text, max 2000 characters.
